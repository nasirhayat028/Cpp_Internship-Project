Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

Instructions:


Choose an Action:

You have three choices: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
Type the number corresponding to your choice:
1 for Rock
2 for Paper
3 for Scissors
See the Result:

The computer will also pick Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
The choices will be shown on the screen.
Determine the Winner:

Rock beats Scissors
Paper beats Rock
Scissors beat Paper
Find Out Who Wins:

If your choice beats the computer's choice, you win!
If it's a tie, the game says so.
If the computer wins, it lets you know.
Repeat or Quit:

After each round, you can choose to play again or quit.
Type 4 to quit the game.
